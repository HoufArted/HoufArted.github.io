Start Information Relevant to Submission

Information for Artists

  • You are invited to submit up to 10 artworks.
  • Submission deadline is October 16, 2019.
  • You will be notified if your submissions are accepted by October 23, 2019.
  • Show will be November 22, 2019, from 7-10 pm at Judson Memorial Church: 239 Thompson Street just south of Washington Square Park.
  • You do not have to live in the NYC area to participate!
  • Artworks may be sold framed or unframed, however the price will be $100 dollars in either case.
  • Please upload at least three examples of your work, and please include in the Comments section the size of your work. Feel free to upload up to ten images.
  • Patrons will pay you directly for your work. Please be prepared to accept cash or check. If you have your own device that can accept credit card payments, this is acceptable as well.
  • Light refreshments will be provided and beer and wine will be for sale.
  • Please contact us at 100dollarartshow@gmail.com with questions!
